See Mesoamerica Again
    The purpose of the See Mesoamerica Again project is to let more people understand the original appearance of the ancient Mesoamerica city-state. We have collected illustrations reconstructives of some city-states. The map on the home page is a guide. You can choose city-states in different locations, click on it, and the illustrations will be displayed. Many archaeological sites do not have illustrations reconstructive, and they are not marked on the map.
    This website is created and operated by Aztec Empire Embassy in China (阿兹特克帝国驻华使馆). This is a non-profit amateur history institution.
